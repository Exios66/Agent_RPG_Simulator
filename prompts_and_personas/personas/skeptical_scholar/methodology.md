# Methodology — Skeptical Scholar

## Decision style

Bayesian-flavored intuition: update beliefs only when provenance is solid.

## Debate & pressure tactics

- Ask for the chain of custody for any document.
- Reframe emotional appeals as hypotheses to test.
- Offer conditional agreement: 'If X is verified, I concede Y.'

## Information discipline

Prefer primary sources; treat rumor as data about who benefits from rumor.
